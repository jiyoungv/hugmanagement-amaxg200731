Security enhancement through decentralized Identifier

DID (Decentralized Identifier)

DID stands for decentralized identifier.

This is a technology that allows individuals to have complete ownership of their personal
information, so it is an innovative identity verification method using blockchain technology
which does not store and manage your information on central servers of any institutions, but it
will be safely saved in your own personal electronic device in encrypted format.
